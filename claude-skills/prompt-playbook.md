## 🗺️ THE COMPLETE PERSONAL BRAND WORKFLOW
> Use this end-to-end every time you're building a personal brand from scratch.
> Follow the steps in order. Don't skip any.

---

### ✅ STEP 0 — ANALYZE INSTAGRAM (Why It's Not Performing)

**What to give Claude:**
- Your Instagram handle
- Browser access (so Claude can open and screenshot the real profile)

**Prompt:**
```
Open my Instagram profile @[handle] in the browser.
Screenshot it and analyze it completely. Give me a brutally honest diagnosis of:

1. Bio — is it clear, specific, and does it tell someone exactly who I am and why to follow me?
2. Content — what patterns do you see? What's consistent, what's scattered?
3. Niche — do I have one? Is it clear from the outside?
4. Engagement — based on what you can see, what's working and what isn't?
5. Aesthetic — does the grid have a consistent look and feel?
6. Posting habits — frequency, consistency, format variety?
7. What I should completely STOP doing?
8. What I should START doing immediately?

Be brutally honest. Don't sugarcoat. I need the truth, not encouragement.
```

**What Claude will do:** Open the profile, take screenshots, analyze bio, content grid, captions, engagement patterns, and deliver a full diagnosis with an action plan.

---

### ✅ STEP 1 — INTERVIEW (Know the Person)

**What to tell Claude:**
```
Before we build my brand, interview me like a journalist.
Ask me one question at a time. Wait for my answer before asking the next one.
Your goal: understand me deeply enough to define my niche, content pillars, and unique angle.
Be curious. Ask follow-up questions when something is interesting.
Start the interview now.
```

**The 10 core questions Claude should ask (one at a time):**

1. Tell me your full story — where are you from, where are you now, and how did you get here?
2. What do you do every day? What are you currently building or working on?
3. What's the hardest thing you've ever been through? How did it shape you?
4. What do you know now that you wish you'd known 3 years ago?
5. What do you wake up at [time] to do — and why can't you stop doing it?
6. What are 3 things you believe that most people in your world would disagree with?
7. Who do you want to reach? Describe your ideal follower — what are they struggling with?
8. What topics could you talk about for hours without getting bored?
9. What's your daily routine? Walk me through a normal day.
10. Where do you want to be in 1 year? What does success look like for you?

**After the interview, prompt:**
```
Based on everything I just told you:
1. What is my niche? Give it a name.
2. What are my 4 content pillars?
3. What is my unique angle — the one thing that makes me different from everyone else in this space?
4. Who is my audience? Describe them specifically.
Be specific. No generic answers.
```

### ✅ STEP 2 — BRAND IDENTITY

**What to tell Claude:**
```
Now create my full brand identity guidelines based on everything we've discussed.
Use the brand-identity skill to generate a complete HTML document including:
- Colors (with HEX codes)
- Typography (display + body fonts)
- Brand personality / vibes
- Target audience (4 segments)
- Voice & tone (3 pillars)
- Tagline (1 primary + 3 alternatives)
- Do's & Don'ts
- Content pillars as services
Save it as [name]-brand-identity.html
```

**Skill to use:** `brand-identity` (triggers automatically when you say "create brand guidelines")

**Output:** A self-contained HTML file you can open in the browser and print to PDF.

---

### ✅ STEP 3 — CONTENT STRATEGY + WEEKLY PLAN

**What to tell Claude:**
```
Now build my 2-week content plan.
Posting schedule:
- Monday, Wednesday, Saturday → Reels (short form, cinematic @jun_yuh style)
- Tuesday, Friday → Carousels (Instagram + LinkedIn)

For each post, create:
- Post title
- Full script (cinematic text overlay format — one line at a time, no voiceover)
- Caption (direct, honest, zero fluff, ends with question)
- Hashtags (mix of niche + broad, max 10)
- B-roll directions
- Publish date and time (7AM for Tue, 7PM for Fri carousels)

My niche: [your niche]
My content pillars: [your pillars]
My story context: [key facts about you]

Then push the full plan to my Notion database.
Notion API key: [your key]
```

**Tips Claude will give you automatically:**
- Best posting times (Tue/Fri for carousels, high-engagement windows)
- Hook formulas per pillar
- CTA strategies (comment triggers, save magnets)
- Caption length per platform

---

### ✅ STEP 4 — ORGANIZE IN NOTION

**What to tell Claude:**
```
Organize the Notion content plan so it's clear week by week.
Sort by Post # ascending.
Each week should follow: Mon → Tue → Wed → Fri → Sat.
Format field: write "Carousel" for carousel posts, keep reel format for video posts.
Platform: Instagram + LinkedIn for carousels, TikTok + YouTube Shorts + Instagram for reels.
```

### 🔔 REMINDERS (If You Forget)

Claude will remind you if you forget to:
- [ ] Define your niche before creating content
- [ ] Add a CTA to every carousel (comment trigger word)
- [ ] Set posting times, not just dates
- [ ] Add LinkedIn as a platform for carousels
- [ ] Create the brand identity HTML before the content plan
- [ ] Push scripts to Notion (don't just save them locally)
- [ ] Refresh your bio monthly with Prompt 5
