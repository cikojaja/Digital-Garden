## 🍳 The Story: Chef by Day, Builder by Night

I am a 26-year-old self-taught developer from Bandung, Indonesia, currently living in Hamburg, Germany. 
- **By Day:** I run high-stress, fast-paced culinary service lines in 10-hour kitchen shifts. 
- **By Night (4 AM):** I self-fund, learn, and build software architectures, SaaS startups, and automated AI pipelines. 

I have **no computer science degree** and **no coding bootcamps**. Everything you see here was learned by building real-world projects from scratch. I bring the speed, discipline, and execution under pressure of a professional kitchen to writing software.
